Finishers Circle is Belize’s premier submission-only tournament series — created to showcase the true essence of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and combat sports. No points. No politics. Just pure competition. Our mission is to bring together the toughest grapplers from across Central America and let skill speak louder than words.
